About StayEz™

Founded in 2020 in Bandung, StayEz.com has grown from a small Indonesian startup to one of the world’s leading digital travel companies. StayEz.com’s mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the world. By investing in the technology that helps take the friction out of travel, StayEz.com seamlessly connects millions of travellers with memorable experiences, incredible places to stay - from homes to hotels and much more. As one of the world’s largest travel marketplaces for both established brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, StayEz.com enables properties all over the world to reach a global audience and grow their businesses. StayEz™ offers more than 100 thousands listings alone of homes, apartments and other unique places to stay.

PT. StayEz.com , registered in Bandung, provides an online accommodation reservation service. It is the owner, controller and manager of the website, www.stayez.com. Internationally, StayEz.com is supported by various local companies – below, you can see the list of our support companies worldwide. It is the job of these support companies to provide in-country support for StayEz.com. The support companies do not provide an online reservations service and they do not own, control, host, manage or maintain the StayEz.com website (or any other website). StayEz.com is domiciled only at its registered office in Bandung and not at the offices of any support companies around the world. The support companies are not authorised to act as StayEz.com’s process or service agents. The contact details of StayEz.com’s Bandung headquarters can be found below under SEA/Indonesia. And the contact details of each of our worldwide support companies is also listed – but remember, you will not be able to make a reservation through any of these offices.
